About Me

Hey there! My name is samir and I'm persuing MCA from NITJSR. I have a passion for building things and solving complex problems through code.

From there, I studied computer science in college and landed my first job as a software engineer shortly after graduating.

I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn, whether that's exploring a new programming language or tackling a tricky bug.
Outside of work, I enjoy hiking and camping in the beautiful parks around San Francisco, as well as reading and watching movies. I'm also a big fan of video games and occasionally still build my own.
If you're interested in learning more about my work or connecting with me, feel free to check out my portfolio or reach out on LinkedIn.
